## Mission Statement

## Vision

A world where location independence doesn't mean technology dependence. Where your digital life travels with you, works offline, and respects your privacy.

**Project Nomad** is the infrastructure for digital freedom.

---

## The Three Pillars

### 1. **Project Nomad** - The Mission
The overarching initiative to build resilient, portable technology solutions for nomadic lifestyles.

### 2. **ARK** - The Software
Autonomous Resilience Kit: A 13-service Docker stack providing:
- AI & LLM capabilities (Ollama, Open WebUI)
- Media streaming & management (Jellyfin, Audiobookshelf)
- File storage & sync (FileBrowser, Syncthing)
- Password management (Vaultwarden)
- System automation (Home Assistant)
- Offline knowledge (Kiwix)

### 3. **Nomad Node** - The Hardware
The physical platform running ARK:
- VM-based (Hyper-V, VirtualBox, Proxmox)
- Future: Dedicated hardware (mini-PC, NUC, custom builds)

## Target Audience

### Primary: **Digital Nomads**
- Remote workers traveling full-time
- Need reliable tech anywhere
- Value privacy and independence
- Budget: $100-$500 for setup

### Secondary: **Off-Grid Enthusiasts**
- Van-lifers, RV travelers, boat dwellers
- Limited/expensive internet access
- Need local services (media, file storage)
- Budget: $150-$1000 for complete setup

### Tertiary: **Privacy Advocates**
- Want self-hosted alternatives to cloud services
- Don't trust Big Tech
- Willing to self-manage infrastructure
- Budget: Flexible

### Emerging: **Developing Regions**
- Areas with unreliable internet
- Need offline educational resources
- Community internet cafes/centers
- Budget: Minimal (hardware donations)
